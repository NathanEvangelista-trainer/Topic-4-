Welcome to the Initial Training for New SMLC Therapists!

In this training, we will focus on two important components of behavior management used at SMLC.

First, we will discuss the Rules of Reinforcement, which guide us in strengthening and encouraging positive behaviors. Next, we will cover the Guidelines for Punishments, emphasizing the proper, ethical, and non-harmful application of consequences to help reduce unwanted behaviors.

These principles are essential to creating a smooth, balanced, and effective therapy approach for our learners.
